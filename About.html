About the Game

MannaDaily is a daily Bible verse guessing game designed to help players grow in their familiarity with Scripture. Each day, players are challenged to identify the books of the Bible from a selection of verses drawn from across Scripture.


Bible Translation

MannaDaily currently uses verses from the English Standard Version (ESV) Bible translation. We hope to expand in the future with additional game modes and versions that incorporate the Catholic and Orthodox biblical canons.


How to Play

Every day features a new set of 10 Bible verses from different books of the Bible.

For each verse:
Read the verse carefully.
Enter the name of the biblical book you believe it comes from.
You have 3 attempts per verse.
The same daily challenge is available to all players.
